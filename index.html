ABOUT SOCCER

Soccer is typically played on a rectangular field with dimensions that can vary but are generally around 100-110 meters in length and 64-75 meters in width. RULES AND REGULATIONS

Soccer is played on a rectangular field with two teams of 11 players each, including a goalkeeper. The objective is to score goals by getting the ball into the opponent's net.Players are not allowed to be offside when the ball is played to them, and fouls result in free kicks or penalty kicks depending on the location on the field. Misconduct can lead to yellow or red cards. The ball can go out of play along the touchline or goal line, resulting in throw-ins or corner kicks, respectively. The Laws of the Game, established by the IFAB, govern all aspects of play, ensuring fairness and consistency.
